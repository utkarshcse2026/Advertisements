Why Smart‑Lighting Matters

Traditional bulbs consume 2–3× more power than controlled LEDs. Automation lets households schedule usage, cut wastage, and improve ambience. Smart lighting also supports accessibility for elderly or people with mobility difficulty.

Detailed Buying Guide

Before buying, compare lumen count (brightness), wattage, compatibility with assistants, and life cycle. Lower watt but higher lumen bulbs deliver max efficiency.

For beginner users, starter kits offer plug‑and‑play installation and safe preset defaults. Advanced users can integrate automation with sensors, door events, sunlight detection and app routines.
